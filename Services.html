FRANCISTOWN JAZZ FESTIVAL

The much anticipated Francistown jazz festival which was scheduled to take place on April 8 has been postponed indefinitely. According to a press release signed by the festival director, Soares Katumbela the decision to postpone came about due to lack of sponsorship to cover costs for service providers which include technical, stage, sound and artists.
